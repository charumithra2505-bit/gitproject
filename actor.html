SK

Sivakarthikeyan (born 17 February 1985), also known by his initials SK, is an Indian actor, playback singer, lyricist, and film producer primarily active in Tamil cinema. He worked as a television presenter before entering films. He is a recipient of two Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and eight SIIMA Awards. Sivakarthikeyan gained attention after winning the stand-up comedy show Kalakka Povathu Yaaru? in 2006 and began hosting various shows on Star Vijay. His breakthrough in Tamil cinema came with the 2012 comedy-drama Marina. Later, he starred in commercially and critically successful films such as Ethir Neechal (2013), Varuthapadatha Valibar Sangam (2013), Namma Veettu Pillai (2019), and Amaran (2024), with the latter becoming one of the top-grossing Tamil films of that year. He launched his own production company, Sivakarthikeyan Productions, and produced several films, including Kanaa (2018).
